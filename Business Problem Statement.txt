A prominent retail organization seeks to gain deeper insights into customer purchasing behavior to enhance overall sales performance, satisfaction, and long-term loyalty. The management team has observed noticeable shifts in buying trends across various demographics, product lines, and sales platforms (online and offline).

They aim to identify the key factors influencing customer decisions — including discounts, product reviews, seasonal trends, and payment preferences — to better understand what drives repeat purchases and engagement.

You have been assigned to analyze the company’s consumer data to address the central question:

“How can the company utilize customer shopping data to uncover patterns, strengthen engagement, and refine marketing and product strategies?”

Project Deliverables

Data Preparation & Modeling (Python):
Clean, preprocess, and structure the raw dataset for analysis by performing data wrangling, feature creation, and integration.

Data Analysis (SQL):
Design structured database tables to simulate business transactions and execute analytical SQL queries to uncover insights related to customer segmentation, loyalty behavior, and purchase drivers.

Visualization & Insights (Power BI):
Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize trends, highlight customer patterns, and enable stakeholders to explore insights through an intuitive interface.

Report & Presentation:
Compile a comprehensive project report summarizing findings, analytical outcomes, and actionable business recommendations. Create a presentation that effectively communicates insights and strategies to business decision-makers.

GitHub Repository:
Maintain a well-organized repository containing all project assets — Python scripts, SQL codes, Power BI dashboards, and documentation — ensuring transparency and reproducibility of the analysis.
